Dr. Aloke Goit is a distinguished dermatologist with an MD in Dermatology and Venereology from Central South University, China, and an MBBS from Zaporozhe State Medical University, Ukraine. He has conducted significant research in dermatology, contributing to the understanding and treatment of various conditions. Dr. Goit's expertise includes emergency care, pediatrics, and specialized training in aesthetic procedures, reflecting his commitment to comprehensive dermatological care and patient welfare.
